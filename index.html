What are we?

"We must act now" - Jacinda Ardern

Carbon Tiptoe is a carbon footprint calculator app designed to educate users about some of the biggest contributors to carbon emissions and to help users see the impact their choices have on the environment. This app is intended for users who have a desire to learn more about how their everyday choices impact the environment and want to take action to mitigate their carbon footprint.

Our mission

Our mission at "Carbon Tiptoe" is to increase awareness about climate change, solar and wind energy, and green lifestyle choices. We provide information and resources about climate change, solar and wind energy, and green lifestyle choices in an effort to increase awareness about these important topics. Our goal is to empower individuals to make informed choices that will help protect our planet and its resources.
